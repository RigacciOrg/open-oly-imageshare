==== Open Oly ImageShare Privacy Policy ====

Last modified: 06 October, 2023.

Open Oly ImageShare is a Free and Open Source program developed 
on a voluntary basis with the sole purpose of being useful to 
those in need.

By using Open Oly ImageShare, you agree that your personal 
information will be handled as described in this Policy.

  * The app does not collect or transmit user data outside the device.

No data is collected by the app and nothing is sent over the 
internet, you can inspect the operation of the program by 
reading the source code available in the repository 
https://github.com/RigacciOrg/open-oly-imageshare
